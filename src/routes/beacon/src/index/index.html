ABSTRACT

When collaborating on ideas for our senior project, we came up with a handful of ideas that were based off of existing projects. Even though we would be building from these ideas and developing our project from scratch, it didn’t feel original. We wanted our project to feel unique compared to the other projects we had already seen. This is what drove us to develop a project from a more artistic and creative viewpoint.

We want to make a unique user experience where they can interact with our piece of art in a multitude of ways. Our project will be a 2m x 1m (6ft x 3ft) canvas with an array of rgb LED strips attached to the back of the canvas. To the user, they will initially see a blank white canvas on the wall. The user will interact with the canvas via an app on a tablet using touch events and gestures. Each touch event the user provides to the tablet will be translated to the canvas, illuminating the LEDs behind the canvas at the corresponding point. A small servo behind the canvas will also apply pressure to the canvas, pushing the canvas outwards making it appear like the users finger is applying pressure to the canvas.
